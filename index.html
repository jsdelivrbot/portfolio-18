ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Dom Bellettini.

I am a Web Developer, Designer and an outside-the-box Thinker.
Everyday, I find something new that I can create with web development. The seemingly unlimited possibilities code provides is almost alarming, and I am hungry to see how far those boundaries stretch. I am obsessed over mastering these skills for my own evil plans to take over the world... did I say that out loud? Anyways... for me, coding is like creating a living, breathing thing someone can interact with.

When I'm not hunched over my laptop looking for that out of place semi-colon, I'm cheering on my favorite sports teams, testing Netflix's "Are you still watching?" tool, or eating an unhealthy amount of wings and bacon (not necessarily at the same time).
